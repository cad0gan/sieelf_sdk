Sound

Detailed Description

Functions for playing and controlling sound-related stuff.

This function is mainly used for audio hooks or playing short sounds/tones. See OBS_* functions for playing audio in the normal way.

Usage: #include <swilib.h> or #include <swilib/sound.h>
